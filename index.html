Lapio — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-16
Summary: Lapio does not require accounts. We log limited technical and click-attribution data when you tap certain outbound partner links, to measure performance and maintain the marketplace.

1. Who operates Lapio

Lapio is operated by Diogo Soares, based in Portugal. Contact: lapio.feedback@gmail.com

2. What data we collect

Lapio is designed to work without user accounts. Depending on how you use the app, we may collect:

2.1 Marketplace and affiliate click tracking

When you tap certain partner offers or “Book now” buttons, we may record:

  • Offer identifier and partner identifier
  • Optional context identifiers (event and/or track)
  • Timestamp
  • Technical information such as user-agent (e.g., device/browser string)
  • Basic attribution parameters from the link (e.g., source and placement)
  • Hashed IP address (if enabled in our tracking infrastructure)

We do not ask for your name, phone number, or payment details. Lapio does not process payments or bookings.

2.2 Data we do not intentionally collect

  • Precise location (GPS)
  • Contacts
  • Health data
  • Government identifiers
  • Advertising identifiers for cross-app tracking

3. Why we process this data

We process the above data to:

  • Measure outbound link usage and marketplace performance
  • Support affiliate attribution and prevent abuse
  • Improve the app and partner listings

4. Legal basis (GDPR)

For affiliate click tracking and marketplace analytics, our legal basis is legitimate interests (GDPR Article 6(1)(f)) — operating and improving the marketplace.

5. Affiliate disclosure

Some links in Lapio are affiliate links. If you book or purchase via those links, Lapio may receive a commission. This does not change the price you pay (unless explicitly stated by the partner).

6. Where the data is processed

We use the following service providers to operate Lapio:

  • Cloudflare (redirect/tracking infrastructure)
  • Supabase (database storage for click logs and marketplace configuration)
  • Apple (App Store distribution and platform services)

7. Data retention

We keep click tracking data only as long as needed for analytics, fraud prevention, and accounting. We may retain aggregated statistics longer than raw logs.

8. International transfers

Some processing may occur outside the EU/EEA depending on provider infrastructure. Where applicable, providers rely on appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses).

9. Your rights

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have rights including:

  • Access to personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Object to processing
  • Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority

To make a request, contact: lapio.feedback@gmail.com

10. Third-party websites

Lapio links to third-party websites and services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies. Lapio is not responsible for third-party sites.

11. Children

Lapio is not intended for children under 16.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Updates will be posted on this page and the “Last updated” date will change.
