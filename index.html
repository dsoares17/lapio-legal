Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Who We Are

Lapio is an independent event discovery application focused on trackdays and motorsport-related services. Lapio is operated by an individual developer based in the European Union. For privacy-related questions, contact: contact@lapio.app

2. What Lapio Does

Lapio allows users to discover motorsport events and related services. Lapio does not organize events, does not process payments, and does not handle bookings. Users are redirected to third-party websites to complete any transactions.

3. Personal Data We Collect

3.1 No Account System

Lapio does not currently require user registration and does not collect names, passwords, or account information.

3.2 Basic Technical Data

We may process limited technical data necessary for app functionality, such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Anonymous usage metrics
  • Crash reports

This data is used solely to maintain and improve the application.

3.3 Affiliate Link Tracking

When you click on certain outbound links, Lapio may receive compensation from third-party organizers or service providers. These third parties may use their own tracking technologies. Lapio does not control third-party tracking once you leave the app.

4. How We Use Data

We use data strictly to:

  • Provide and maintain the app
  • Improve event discovery experience
  • Monitor performance and reliability
  • Measure outbound referral activity

5. Data Storage

Lapio uses Supabase (EU-based infrastructure where available) to store event and partner data. Lapio does not store sensitive personal information.

6. Third-Party Services

Lapio may redirect users to external websites (event organizers, coaches, rental providers, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. Users should review those websites' privacy policies independently.

7. Data Retention

Technical analytics data is retained only as long as necessary to improve and maintain the service. Lapio does not maintain user accounts or long-term personal profiles.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Object to processing
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, contact: contact@lapio.app

9. Children’s Privacy

Lapio is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any updates.
